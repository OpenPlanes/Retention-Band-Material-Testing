# Retention-Band-Material-Testing
Material durability study of elastic retention bands used in robotic storage systems. Developed a test plan comparing TPU, EPDM, and spandex-based bands using cyclic fatigue, creep, tensile, and environmental testing to evaluate long-term stretch, failure modes, and material performance.

ANSYS Structural Analysis

Finite element analysis was performed in ANSYS to evaluate the mechanical behavior of retention band material coupons under tensile loading. Hyperelastic material models were applied to simulate elastomer deformation, and simulations were used to compare stress distribution, strain localization, and deformation across candidate materials. Results helped identify potential fatigue-critical regions and guide material downselection.

Boundary Conditions
- Bottom face: fixed
- Top face: displacement applied (50% elongation)
Outputs
- Equivalent stress
- Total deformation
- Principal strain
The principal strain is important for elastomers.
